#1. Thoughtful Topper (Double IPA)

ABV: 7.2%

IBU: 93

Base Malts (~80%): 2-Row, German Pilsner

Adjuncts (~20%): Chit, Wheat, Honey Malt

Hops: Warrior, Columbus, Amarillo, Simcoe, Cascade, Citra

Double Dry Hop: Simcoe, Cascade, Columbus

Yeast Strain: LalBrew New England

Tasting Notes: Super Dank, Citrusy notes, followed by a blast of stone fruit, mango and more citrus on top of a pillowy bed of honey and wheat. Hop character.

Tastes Like: Heady Topper

#3. The Coffeebreak (Coffee Stout)

ABV: 5.2%

IBU: 33

Base Malts (75%): 2-Row

Adjuncts (~25%): Roasted Barley, Flaked Barley, Flaked Oats

Hops: Simcoe, Citra

Yeast Strain: Nottinghams

Tasting Notes: A rush of coffee straight to the back of the throat, middling with some full bodied milky/oat flavor, finishing with an acidic coffee taste.
